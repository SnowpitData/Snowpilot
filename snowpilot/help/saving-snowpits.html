When you have entered all the snowpit profile information you need, you may 'Save Snowpit and Preview.' This will give you a preliminary preview of the Snowpit Profile image. If you need to make any updates, you can still 'Edit Snowpit'; but if everything looks good, you can 'Save, Lock, and Download Snowpit'. Download formats currently include jpg, png, and snowpilot xml.

After being saved and locked, the snowpit can no longer be edited, and is saved to the AvScience database.

Org Admins have special access to edit snowpits created by users within their group; but only until the snowpit is locked and saved. Read more.
